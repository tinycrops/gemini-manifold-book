Chapter 2

The Next Experiment Is Waiting

Chapter 1 proved that one ASCII artifact can live as text, image, and image+text in one shared space. Chapter 2 will widen the test set and start asking whether an LLM can learn a stable communicative grammar through ASCII, rather than just producing isolated beautiful examples.

The next likely direction is to add multiple objects, compare retrieval behavior across categories, and look for early signs of an ASCII-native vocabulary emerging inside the manifold.
