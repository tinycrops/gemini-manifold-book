A Living Research Book

The Book of ASCII Communication

This site documents an unfolding project: learning how to teach LLMs to communicate with ASCII art as text, as image, and as fused multimodal structure. Each chapter freezes one meaningful experimental moment so the ideas can accumulate in public instead of disappearing into transient terminal sessions.

Chapter 1

Saint Basil's Cathedral in Three Semantic Forms

The first chapter captures one Cathedral as three separate objects: ASCII text, raster image, and image+text. It is the first clean glimpse of how Gemini Embedding 2 relates those forms inside one shared manifold.

Thesis

ASCII is not decoration here.

The project assumption is simple: ASCII can be a medium that holds structure, style, and intention in ways that are legible to both people and models. The book exists to test that assumption chapter by chapter.
